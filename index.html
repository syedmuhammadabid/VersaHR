About us

Over the last 20 years business has become more global than ever. Christian Schmidt realized the potential of the newly globalized economy for human resources and created Versa, building on his career of experience in the field.

Schmidt realized that while the global economy was becoming more forward-thinking, rapid, and flexible, human resource departments and companies were not keeping pace. Schmidt founded Versa to give employers both access to a wider pool of potential employees from all around the world, and find the best candidates from all that diverse potential. A truly modern human resources company would unleash unlimited possibilities for talented and driven individuals no matter where they are located.

Versa provides outreach and recruitment services for employers looking for the cutting-edge in human resources, at competitive rates and fast turnover. A cross-industry employment agency,Versa works in all job markets.

Versa realizes that in today's economy, freelance and project-oriented employment is increasing in value. As demand for specialization and flexibility grow hand-in-hand, Versa's expertise in freelance and project-based employment gives you a versatile solution in these high-yield areas. Versa offers clients flexible work arrangements to fit your needs, whether contingent recruiting or boutique retained executive search.
